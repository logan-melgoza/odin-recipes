Buffalo Wings Recipe

Description

This recipe will walk you through how to make quick and easy Baked Buffalo Wings. These are the perfect snack to share at any event and will definetly be a fan favorite!

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup butter, melted

  • 1/3 cup Frank's RedHot Orginal Cayenne Pepper Hot Sauce, 12 oz

  • 2 1/2 pounds chicken wing pieces

Steps

  1. PREHEAT oven to 450F. Mix butter and RedHot Sauce in medium bowl to make the buffalo wing sauce; set aside.

  2. ARRANGE chicken wings on large foil-lined pan. Bake on lowest oven rack for 30 minutes or until crispy, turning once.

  3. TOSS chicken wings in sauce mixture until well coated. Serve with blue cheese and celery, if desired.
